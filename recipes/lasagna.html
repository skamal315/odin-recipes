Lasagna

Description

Lasagne are a type of pasta, possibly one of the oldest types, made of very wide, flat sheets. Either term can also refer to an Italian dish made of stacked layers of lasagne alternating with fillings of beef and cheese.

Ingredients

  • pasta
  • sauce
  • cheese
  • ground beef
  • onion

Steps

  1. Prepare a pot of water to boil pasta
  2. Heat sauce in sauce pan
  3. Lightly warm beef crumbles in olive oil on stovetop
  4. Make a cheese paste
  5. Place boiled pasta in baking dish, layer with cheese and beef
  6. Place in oven for 40 minutes at 350 Degrees
