S'Mores

Description

A s'more is a campfire treat popular in the United States and Canada, consisting of one or more toasted marshmallows and a layer of chocolate sandwiched between two pieces of graham cracker.

Ingredients

  • pasta
  • sauce
  • cheese
  • ground beef
  • onion

Steps

  1. Prepare a pot of water to boil pasta
  2. Heat sauce in sauce pan
  3. Lightly warm beef crumbles in olive oil on stovetop
  4. Make a cheese paste
  5. Place boiled pasta in baking dish, layer with cheese and beef
  6. Place in oven for 40 minutes at 350 Degrees
